Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how James & Elise, the organizers of the wedding, collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when you respond through this website.

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by James & Elise for the sole purpose of managing details for our upcoming wedding.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit this form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Your Name(s): To identify you and your party.
  • Postal Address: If you indicate you are attending, this is collected to send you a physical wedding invitation.
  • Attendance Status: Whether you are attending or not.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect this information directly from you when you voluntarily fill out and submit the form on this website, and store it in a Google Sheet.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your responseand manage our guest list.
  • To send you a physical wedding invitation if you indicate you are attending.
  • To contact you with essential updates or questions regarding the wedding, if necessary.

5. How We Share Your Information

We value your privacy and will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your information will only be shared with:

  • N8N Workflow: The data you submit is securely sent via a Netlify serverless function to our personal n8n workflow for automated processing and organization of RSVPs. This workflow is managed by James and is not publicly accessible.
  • Postal Services: Your postal address will be used for mailing your physical invitation via standard postal services.
  • Google Sheets:for guest list management

6. Data Storage and Security

The information you submit through the form is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. It is then processed by a secure Netlify serverless function, which forwards it to our n8n workflow. Once received by n8n, it is stored in a Google Sheet. We take reasonable steps to protect the information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the duration of the wedding planning process and to send out the physical invitations. Once the wedding has passed and invitations have been sent, we will delete the data within 6 months.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information from our records.
  • Withdraw Consent: If you previously consented to any specific use of your data, you can withdraw that consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Bracey2b@gmail.com.

9. Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

10. Hosting Information (Netlify)

This website is hosted on Netlify, which provides the static site hosting and serverless functions that power this RSVP form. Netlify may collect data related to website traffic and usage for operational purposes. Please refer to the Netlify Privacy Policy for details on their data collection practices.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
